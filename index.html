About Me

I started learning programming in the spring semester of my second year of college. Up until that point, I had been on the pre-med track with a pending major in Psychology. I had decided that I wanted to experience different fields of study and programming seemed like something innovative and fascinating.

The thing that got me hooked was that programming is both rigid and flexible. You have to be exact with your coding (or else it won't work), but you also have many ways of going about finding a solution and writing your program. I also enjoyed learning how to think like a programmer, even to the point when I started thinking of algorithms for things in day-to-day life. My fascination with the field of Computer Science, combined with my love of Psychology, has pushed me to explore full stack development in order to optimize my programs efficiently and purposefully for my user.

I am currently pursuing a career that integrates my interest in Computer Science and Psychology in fields such as Artificial Intelligence and Human-Computer Interaction. I hope that you enjoy exploring my website!
